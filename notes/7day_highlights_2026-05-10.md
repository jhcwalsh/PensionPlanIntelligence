## Investment Policy and Governance Changes

**TRS Texas** (~$200B, valued at **$235.2 billion as of December 31, 2025**) proposed amending its Investment Policy Statement to raise the Total Manager Organization Market Value Limit for Public Markets from **3% to 5%** of the total fund, and the IIC Approval Authority per Manager Organization from **6% to 8%**, with the change attributed to two unnamed managers approaching or exceeding existing limits due to strong performance rather than active allocation decisions ([source](?doc=4000)). The Governance Committee voted unanimously to recommend the amendment to the full board ([source](?doc=4000)).

**VRS** (~$110B) transitioned its International Stock Fund to a **BlackRock MSCI ACWI ex-USA, ex-China, ex-Hong Kong IMI Index Fund**, moving approximately **$376.7 million** with no change in management fees, to comply with the VRS Foreign Adversaries Policy ([source](?doc=4030)). The DCPAC also noted that auto escalation for the Hybrid 457 Plan raised voluntary participation to **96%**, with an opt-out rate of **1.3%** ([source](?doc=4030)).

**BERS** (~$7B) authorized adding seven managers to a transition management services pool for public market transactions and initiated an Equity Index Notice of Search for new public equity index manager(s), both per recommendation of **Segal Marco Advisors** and the Comptroller ([source](?doc=4012)). The board also unanimously authorized divestment from for-profit prison companies (approved at the April 21, 2026 meeting) ([source](?doc=4012)).
