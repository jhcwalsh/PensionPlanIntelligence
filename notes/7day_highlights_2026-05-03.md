## Real Estate Strategy and Manager Actions

**LACERS** (~$25B) presented its Real Estate FY2026-27 Strategic Plan at the May 12 Investment Committee meeting, recommending up to **$130 million** in Core/Core Plus commitments and up to **$200 million** in Non-Core commitments (targeting **$50–65 million** per fund), as the fund works to close the gap between its current **5.3%** real estate allocation and the **7.0%** target ([source](?doc=3677)). ## Manager Hires, Searches, and Consultant Changes

**CCPF** (~$12B) approved **Driehaus Capital Management LLC** and **Causeway Capital Management LLC** for international small cap equity mandates of up to **$225 million** each, by a **7-2** vote, following an RFP process in which 37 firms responded and three were named finalists ([source](?doc=3706)).
