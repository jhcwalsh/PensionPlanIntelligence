## Portfolio Strategy and Risk Framework

SIB-ND's board pack provided the period's most substantive strategy update. The plan is progressing toward **Advanced Funds Management**, separating alpha from beta exposures in a three-phase roadmap culminating in overlay and portable alpha implementation. A Fixed Income 2.0 review is underway following 2025's success with Equity 2.0, which returned **18.0%** versus a **17.1%** benchmark. Staff are also exploring AI tools for investment monitoring. New Trade Error and Rebalancing Error policies were adopted following a prior execution incident.

**LACERA**'s Credit and Risk Mitigation Committee reviewed its **$22.26 billion** Risk Reduction and Mitigation portfolio (24% of total fund target), which outperformed across all periods: **6.8%** vs. **6.0%** benchmark (1-year), **5.1%** vs. **5.0%** (3-year), and **1.5%** vs. **1.1%** (5-year) through February 28, 2026. **Virginia Retirement System** (**$110B**) Investment Advisory Committee advanced recommendations to its Board of Trustees on benchmark modifications and asset allocation adjustments, with a foreign adversaries exposure update also on the agenda.
