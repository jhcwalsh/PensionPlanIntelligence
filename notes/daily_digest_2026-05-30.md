## Kentucky Public Pensions Authority
**Kentucky Public Pensions Authority**

On governance, the committee reviewed an IPS amendment, compliance and proxy voting reports, a Solovis platform contract update, and the June 3 KRS special-called meeting was convened solely to review investment-related policies with outside counsel Reinhart, with no investment actions taken at that session.
