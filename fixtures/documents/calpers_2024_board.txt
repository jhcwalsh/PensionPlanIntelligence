Investment Performance Q4 2023
The Total Fund returned 6.7% during Q4 2023, versus a benchmark return
of 6.4%. Public equity contributed the most, returning 11.8%. Fixed
income trailed at 4.2%. Private equity, marked one quarter in arrears,
returned 5.1%.

[Page 4]
Asset Allocation Review
Current allocations remain within policy targets. No rebalancing
recommended this quarter. Real assets exposure will be reviewed at the
next meeting.

[Page 12]
ITEM 5: Investment Consulting Services
Staff recommends issuing a Request for Proposal for general investment
consulting services. The Board's contract with Wilshire Associates
expires December 31, 2024. RFP would be released March 15, 2024 with
responses due May 1, 2024. Estimated annual fee $1.2M. The selection
committee will comprise three trustees and the Chief Investment Officer.

[Page 13]
The proposed scope of services includes asset allocation review, manager
monitoring, performance reporting, and ad-hoc strategic projects. Staff
will return with finalist recommendations at the August 2024 meeting.

[Page 28]
ITEM 6: Global Equity Manager Search — Finalist Interviews
The search committee interviewed three finalists for the active global
equity mandate: BlackRock, State Street Global Advisors, and Vanguard.
Mandate size $500 million. Award decision expected at the May 2024
meeting. The incumbent manager Northern Trust will continue to manage
the portfolio until transition is complete.

[Page 29]
Each finalist presented their investment philosophy, fee proposals, and
performance attribution. Staff will publish a detailed scoring memo
prior to the May meeting.
