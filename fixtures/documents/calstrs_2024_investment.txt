ITEM 3: Actuarial Services Contract Award
The Investment Committee voted 7-0 to award the five-year actuarial
services contract to Cheiron, effective July 1, 2024. Cheiron replaces
the incumbent Segal Consulting. Annual contract value approximately
$2.5 million. The award concludes the search process initiated in
August 2023.

[Page 9]
Staff will work with Cheiron on the transition plan over the next
quarter. Segal's final deliverables include the FY 2024 valuation
report due September 30, 2024.
